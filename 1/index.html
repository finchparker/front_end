To the founders of today and pioneers of tomorrow

I started Hashed with my partners in mid 2017 because we saw an opportunity for blockchain to not only reinvent the global financial system, but the very fabric of the internet itself.

We believe this is one of the most consequential moments in human history — an opportunity to overlay the power of digital networks onto human ones, and to usher in a future where technology empowers individuals, and not the other way around.

At Hashed, our mission is to accelerate the mass adoption of blockchain by investing and empowering a new wave of entrepreneurs and innovators who are building this future.

We’re looking for blockchain geeks, dreamers and trend setters passionate about navigating the ever complicated cross section of engineering, game theory and economics.

To date, we have backed over 40 visionary founders all over the globe who are pioneering this industry. And we are just getting started.

We are Hashed, and we are looking forward to partnering with you to build the decentralized future.

Simon KimCEO and Partner, Hashed

Hashed is a global, early stage venture fund focused on backing founders who are pioneering the future of blockchain and cryptocurrency. Hashed is based in Seoul and San Francisco.
