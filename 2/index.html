Faraday's Mission

Faraday is a cryptocurrency and blockchain advisory backed by on-the-ground research. We help companies make their way into Korea — one of the most opaque but significant cryptocurrency and blockchain scenes in the world — by drawing on locally embedded knowledge and expansive networks.

The Team

Faraday is founded by former military strategy and policy analysts with first-hand experience in forging transnational partnerships. The founders have their experience in serving high-level figures in the Joint Chiefs of Staff of Republic of Korea and Strategy and Planning Directorate with strong bilingual abilities. It also retains strong tech advisors in the company, leveraging the most of the current and past information in and out of the Korean market.

Products

Advisory

Provides informed insights and performs relevant services leveraging expansive networks to enhance partners' presence in the Korean market. Designs pathways for partners and act upon them to navigate through complex environment.

Research

Studies current phenomenon in the Korean market and emerging technologies to deduce meaningful implications. Also, performs tailored research tasks to map pathways that would bring our partners into desired end-state.

Media

Community

Cultivates education communities to provide a platform for prospective industry leaders to develop competence for emerging technology and industries.
